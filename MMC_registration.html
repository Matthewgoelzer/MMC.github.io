Packing List

Clothing Suggestions

  • T-shirt
  • Jeans
  • Mid-thigh shorts
  • Sweatshirts
  • Jackets for cool mornings & evenings
No short shirts, short shorts, halter-tops, etc.

Things to Bring

  • Money for the snack bar ($15)
  • toilet articles
  • soap
  • towel
  • toothbrush & paste
  • comb
  • brush
  • Bible
  • hiking shoes
  • flashlight
  • pen
  • notebook
  • warm sleeping bag
  • pillow
  • insect repellent
  • fishing pole
  • worms
  • hat
  • sunscreen
  • water bottle

Do Not Bring

  • Music devices not used for services
  • TV
  • electronic games
  • fireworks
  • illegal drugs
  • tobacco
  • alcohol
  • knives
  • firearms
  • cell phones
  • water toys
  • explosives
  • matches/lighters
